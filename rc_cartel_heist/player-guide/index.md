# Player Guide

## Overview

The Cartel Island Heist is a multi-stage robbery where you travel to a cartel-controlled island, fight through armed guards, and steal valuable loot from containers, crates, and the underground bunker.

## Getting to the Island

Travel to the island by boat. A hint blip on the map marks the island's location. Approach the island and dock to begin the heist.

## Heist Progression

### Stage 1: Island Exterior

1. **Pick up EMP Blockers** — Find EMP blocker props around the satellite dishes. Pick them up and use them to hack the satellite signals.

2. **Hack Satellites** — Approach each satellite and place the EMP blocker to disable the signal.

3. **Cut Electricity** — Locate the electrical panel and use wire cutters to cut the power. You must have wire cutters in your inventory.

4. **Loot Shipping Containers** — Open the containers on the island. Some require thermite to break the lock.

### Stage 2: Underground Bunker

Once the entrance conditions are met (EMPs hacked, electricity cut, containers looted), the bunker entrance becomes accessible.

1. **Enter the Bunker** — Use the entrance to go underground.

2. **Find the Access Card** — Pick up the access card inside the bunker.

3. **Swipe the Card** — Use the card at the card reader. This triggers a minigame — complete it successfully to proceed.

4. **Loot Crates** — Open crates using melee weapons (faster) or bare hands (slower). Loot the contents.

5. **Loot Jewellery Tables** — Pick up jewellery items from the display tables.

6. **Open Interior Containers** — Use thermite to break container locks inside the bunker and loot the contents.

### Stage 3: Sell the Loot

After escaping the island:

1. **Find the Buyer** — A blip on the map marks the buyer's location.
2. **Sell your items** — Interact with the buyer to sell all heist loot for cash. Prices vary each reset.

## Items

| Item | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| EMP Blocker | Hack satellite dishes |
| Wire Cutters | Cut electrical panel wires |
| Thermite | Break container locks |
| Entrance Key | Access the island (spawns on the island) |
| Access Card | Swipe at card reader for bunker access |

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `E` | Interact with objects: pick up items, open crates/containers, use card reader, sell to buyer |

::: tip
When using targeting mode, interactions happen through the third-eye system instead of the `E` key.
:::

## Tips

- Bring melee weapons (hatchet, battleaxe, crowbar) to open crates faster
- A helicopter spawns on the island for a quick escape
- The heist resets periodically — loot respawns after the timer expires
- Watch out for cartel NPCs — they are armed and dangerous
